Features

Zipkick is a travel search and booking site that brings unique, relevant results to users across all platforms. With limited space on mobile devices, we aim to remove the clutter of irrelevant search results and provide information based on customer preference ranking. Users now have the ability to find what's relevant without scroll bars, radio buttons or searching thousands of different options through a simple and repeatable process.

  • Search & Book in just a couple of clicks
  • Reliable and Secure Platform
